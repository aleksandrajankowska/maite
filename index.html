About My Work

Montreal artist Maïté Côté creates dreamlike portraits of women that contemplate the mystical elements of femininity. Her works use the strong eye contact of her subjects to establish a connection with the viewer into the emotional realm of the piece. Embracing the excitement of rapid creation in her first season with Art Battle, Maïté painted amongst the cities best live artists to claim her place in the National Championship. 2018 & 2019.
